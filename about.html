About Me

Hello, my name is Ian Yeung. I am a computer science major and a junior undergraduate at NJIT. I am also a member of the NJIT chapter of Association for Computing Machinery. My hobbies and interests involve designing games and game levels. Unfortunately, I have no work experience. This is why I am looking for an internship or co-op opportunity. What I hope to gain from such a position is not only job experience but also to learn about working with a group in a company setting. Computer programming is rarely a one-man job. As a result, collaboration and cooperation is very important. Finding an opportunity to work with people in a large group can only be found in an office setting. I hope you will consider me for this job opportunity.
